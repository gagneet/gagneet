Philosophy

Guiding Principles

"Ship value, not features"
Every engineering decision should trace back to a measurable user or business outcome.
"Automation over repetition"
If a process can be automated, it should be. Manual work is technical debt waiting to happen.
"Security by design, not by chance"
Security must be baked into architecture from day one — not retrofitted after an incident.
